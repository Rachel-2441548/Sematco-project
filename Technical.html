1.Technical Department

Key Responsibilities:

  1. Ensure test procedures in both drilling and laboratory departments comply with international standards (ASTM, BS, DIN, ISO, ACI).
  2. Train staff to conduct newly introduced tests according to standard requirements.
  3. Implement health and safety training across all technical personnel.
  4. Review and approve all test reports prior to client submission.
  5. Prepare and issue all technical reports.
  6. Perform structural analysis for requested building evaluations.
  7. Archive all technical reports in both physical and electronic formats.
  8. Ensure consistent adherence to health and safety protocols by all staff.
  9. Offer clients access to qualified third-party inspectors.

Scope of Work:

  1. Design and supervision of soil investigation programs.
  2. Design and supervision of dewatering system.
  3. Design of soil improvement techniques, i.e., grouting, soil reinforcement, compaction.
  4. Design of foundation system both shallow and deep.
  5. Evaluation of soil bearing capacity.
  6. Evaluation of soil parameters.
  7. Evaluation of concrete structures.
  8. Design of retaining walls, shoring systems and embankments.
  9. Assessment of environmental impact for new projects.
  10. Design and supervision of bituminous mixes.
  11. Carry third party inspection at sites in compliance with client requirements to watch, inspect or supervise the works.

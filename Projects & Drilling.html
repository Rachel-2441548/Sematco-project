2. Projects & Drilling Department

Department Overview:
This department is responsible for all site-related soil work—both onshore and offshore. It ensures maintenance of drilling rigs and tools, and is staffed with qualified professionals including mechanical engineers, experienced drillers, drivers, mechanics, and support personnel. The department operates seven drilling rigs to support the following tasks and services.

Key Tasks:

  1. Perform all site work related to soil investigation, both onshore and offshore.
  2. Conduct maintenance operations on all drilling rigs.
  3. Maintain an adequate stock of tools for smooth and efficient operations.

Scope of Work:

  1. Conduct geotechnical boreholes onshore and offshore to various depths.
  2. Perform Standard Penetration Tests (SPT) and Cone Penetration Tests (CPT) for soil strength evaluation.
  3. Carry out pressure meter tests.
  4. Collect both disturbed and undisturbed soil samples.
  5. Collect water samples for analysis.
  6. Perform field permeability tests.
  7. Install water wells and dewatering systems.
  8. Drill holes (vertical, horizontal, or inclined) for the installation of anchors, cathodic protection probes, and related works.
